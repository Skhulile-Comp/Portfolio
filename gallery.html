Table Mountain

Table Mountain is a flat-topped mountain overlooking the city of Cape Town in South Africa. It is a popular tourist destination and offers stunning views of the city and the surrounding area.

Durban is a coastal city in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. It is known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant culture.

Drakensberg Mountains

The Drakensberg is a mountain range in South Africa and Lesotho. It is known for its dramatic landscapes and rich biodiversity.

Drakensberg, South Africa

Robben Island

Robben Island is a small island off the coast of Cape Town, South Africa. It is a UNESCO World Heritage site and was a political prison during the apartheid era.

Cape Town, South Africa

Garden Route

The Garden Route is a scenic stretch of coastline in South Africa, known for its pristine beaches, lush forests, and vibrant towns.

Garden Route, South Africa
